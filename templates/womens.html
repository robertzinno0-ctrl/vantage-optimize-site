Common Questions

Women Ask Us…

What does a coach actually do?

Coaches educate, support lifestyle changes, guide nutrition and habits, and provide accountability. They do not prescribe, diagnose, or recommend specific therapies — that's your provider's role.

How is my protocol determined?

A licensed IHN provider reviews your labs and health intake to create a personalized prescription protocol. All therapeutic decisions are made by your provider — never by your coach.

Is this safe for women over 50?

Our Women's 50+ Protocol is built around safety, personalization, and sustainability. All protocols are provider-supervised, lab-guided, and adjusted based on your individual response.
